Title

If you are using this website in a language other than english, the translations will most likely be inaccurate. I suggest using "Custom Mode", then "Add Custom Word" to create your own questions for a better experience.

Through this, one might theoretically use this website to learn words from any language, by simply inserting the desired foreign language into "Japanese Word". and one's native tongue into "English Translation". Please research the direct translation carefully before adding it.

This website aims to teach you the meaning of Japanese words through brute force learning (Repetition).

One can learn every word on this website in under three hours of brute force learning.

However to remember them, one should try returning to the website from time to time until they stick.

About

This website is heavily inspired by Kana, and I owe the concept and most of the ideas to them, their website is also much more polished and well made than mine.

Speaking of Kana if you do not yet know Hiragana, please learn it at that website, it is (largely) required to use this site, and Hiragana takes very little time to learn with that site (3 hours).

Do not press "Next Round" until you become comfortable with every word shown in the current round. You don't need to master them yet, as they'll always show up in practice rounds later along with every other word you've learnt, but you should know them fairly well.

Speaking the words and phrases aloud as you type them helps a lot with memorization, as does playing the sound for them (the pronunciation for each word plays by default, and you can press a button to play it again. If you'd like to disable this, there's an option to do so in settings).

If you notice any mistakes or issues i would appreciate if you let me know so i could resolve them. if you encounter such, or have any questions, suggestions, feedback, or otherwise any wish to contact me, i'll add the support email here: learnjapanesewords.site@gmail.com

Apologies if I take a while to respond.

I ask that you don't use AdBlock on this website, I put the ads to the side and they won't pop up or interrupt your learning, I would appreciate this unless it seriously inconveniences you.

Without (much) further ado, enjoy! Hopefully you find this website helpful and stick with it until you learn every word!

Practice

Includes 11 rounds of pre-set Japanese words to learn, this is the default mode and intended starting place for new users.

Words used in brackets are not required to be typed in, and only exist to clarify the words meaning. For example, when shown: Please (Request) simply type in "please".

"Custom Mode", as its name suggests, allows you to customize what words you'll be learning, offering a selection of all pre-set words found in brute force mode to go into each custom round (and consequently each practice round).

You can also add your own words through the "Add Custom Word" button, which is as simple as entering in the Japanese text for a word (or theoretically any language), and then its translation (the correct answer).

Do your best not to include very long words or phrases, as typing them out can become a hassle, and ensure that the translation you researched and added is accurate.

More content will be added in the future.
